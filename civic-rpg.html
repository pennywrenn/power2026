The Stakes

Project 2025 was a plan. Power 2026 is the answer. Not from any party. From the people who understand that democracy is not a condition — it's a practice.

The only project that matters here is the American Project. You are already in it. The question is what role you play.

This game runs you through five decision points — the actual choices real citizens face between now and November 2026. You have 20 seconds per decision. Democracy doesn't wait.
